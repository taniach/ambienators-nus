Welcome!

SmartAmbient Sensor system is made for YOU - the manager of a workplace, school, or office or anyone who wants to keep track of environmental data in an area. No more worrying about wasting electricity, now you can nail the culprits quickly and save your wallet!

Get real-time data and history

The SmartAmbient Sensor System operates on an arduino, which requires very little power, as compared to competing sensor products. You can get real time information about temperature, light intensity and presence of motion and view current data and history.

Convenient notification system

You can choose to receive notifications either for every time interval (e.g every 3 hours), or for customised parameters, such as alerts when the temperature falls below 22°C.

Access from anywhere you want

Any device, including your Mac, PC, tablet, iPhone, Android, or even your Windows phone, can be used to access sensor data and history, from anywhere in the world!
